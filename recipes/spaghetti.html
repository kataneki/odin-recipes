Spaghetti

Description

Fast, and easy carb heavy meal. Yay.

Ingredients

  • Pasts (ideally angel hair pasta)
  • Sauce (preference up to the individual)
  • 2 pinches Salt
  • Water
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Fill pan with water to desired height.
  2. Add salt and oil in water in pan.
  3. Boil water on high heat on stove. (Don't burn yourself)
  4. Heat up sauce in seperate pan on stove on low heat.
  5. Once water is boiling, drop heat to Medium-high and put in noodles.
  6. Set timer dependent on type of noodle. (about 5 american minutes for angel hair)
  7. Once timer is done, turn off heat on noodles and pour the pasta noodles into a strainer inside of the sink to avoid spilling on the floor.
  8. Once pasta is drained of majority of agua, plate the noodles.
  9. Pour desired amount of pasta sauce on plated noodles and turn off heat.
  10. Top with desired condiments.
  11. Eat and be happy. (Hopefully)
